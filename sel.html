Rebranding for the Stage Critique blog Sinais em Linha.

Ricardo
Guerreiro
Barroso

An open ended approach to Sinais em Linha's identity, that reflects both its academic backbone and the innovative and expansive voice it will bring to the table with this new iteration.

Mainly focusing on Theatre Review, Sinais em Linha was an active critique platform throughout the 10's, but went silent in 2020. This new start will see its horizons broaden, with themes spanning across other artistic media and article formats. It was in this context that I was invited to develop a new image and system for the project, simple and yet adaptable to a range of editorial possibilities, to be explored in the future.

The brand has been implemented already in social media, and the blog will follow suit soon. I'm currently working on its new website.
