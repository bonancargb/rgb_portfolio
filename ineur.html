Graphic language and elements for Marta Fonseca's tour Isto não é um Recital.

Ricardo
Guerreiro
Barroso

As a classical guitarist Marta Fonseca knows the ins and outs of classical music concerts and recitals. For that reason, for her new tour there was an urge to break the mold, going against what one conventionally associates with the genre.

Asked to develop an image that would reflect that rebelious act and could yet retain some formality, I developed posters and a panflet for the tour mixing grainy visuals - photos courtesy of Beatriz Felício - with simple typography and some blackletter details.

With the beginning of 2026 a new wave of concerts has come, this time in Amsterdam, where Marta is currently living. For the new dates a visual language that reflected this new chapter but kept some of the principles of the previous one was created.

Using Beatriz Felício's photos of one of last year's final gigs as a starting block once again, this time giving them an even more central role, I embraced their orange undertones to invoke the Dutch identity, constrasting them with white and green typography and visual elements. Simultaneously, a simplified depiction of the circuit between the venues where the performances will take place was integrated, as well as some pixelized tulips to add to the theme.
