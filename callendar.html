With Christmas right around the corner the same old question popped up again: what should I give my family for Christmas? 2025 wasn't the best of years money wise, so the Holidays' spirit was at an all time low. That's when it suddenly hit me: ... I'm a designer, come on, I can make something myself.

Truth is, thanks to that, I ended up with these callendars - two versions, a regular and a colouring book for my little nephews, both filled with pictures and drawings of silly objects, landscapes and situations I collected throughout 2025. Not only did they solve my problem, but also turned out to be a way of showing how much I cherish the people around me through what I love to do. Can't wait to see what I'll be up to next Christmas!

Oh, and if you're still on the lookout for a callendar, let me know.
